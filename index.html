Our Mission:

Our mission is to find the perfect opportunities for you. From volunteering in a soup kitchen to planting trees in local gardens, we make it easy. VolunTree caters to your community service expertise. All you need to do is categorize your availability and want to branch out in the fields of environmental causes, poverty, elderly homes, etc. We provide you with all the available options that we think would be a great fit for you to give back to the community, and show all the information from the organization. We are rooting for people of all ages to help spread our message and be active.

Your Incentive:

You earn badges by the amount that you help out in your community. Everyone starts out with a seedling badge, and as you volunteer more, your tree grows. These badges serve as milestones to reflect your progress and your amazing mark on the world!
